GYA KHO

Thupka or Gya thuk is a well-known soup of northeastern states. Sikkim, a state of India & is also part of northern states very popular for it's cuisine in northeastern states. Sharing Sikkim's simple yet comforting soup made of spice paste, noodle & veggies.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. Carrot sliced thin in to equal parts .
2. water or vegetable broth .
3. oil.
4. Rice noodles.
5. Salt to taste .
6. lemon juice.
7. onion chopped.
8. Tomatoes chopped.
9. Garlic.

Method Of Cocking

1. Grind the ingredients under grind to smooth paste.
2. Heat pan with oil. Add paste to pan.
3. Fry it till the raw smell goes off.
4. Add vegetable broth to pan & cook it on low heat for 10mins.
5. To the broth add carrots & cook for few minutes. Switch off heat.
6. Add salt & lemon juice to soup & adjust taste if needed.
7. Heat a deep vessel with full of water with little salt. Bring water to boil & switch off heat. Add noodles to vessel. Stir occasionally if needed. After 5mins drain water & wash noodles with cold water.
8. Transfer soup in to serving bowls & top with cooked rice noodles.
9. Garnish with spring onions & serve right away.
