ROASTED VEGETABLE

Roasted vegetables are famous because they are tender, creamy, crisp, and crunchy, and have a delicious caramelization. Roasting vegetables at a high heat releases water from the vegetables and caramelizes their natural sugars, which intensifies their flavors and makes them sweeter.
