ALMOND MILK RAVA KESARI

Almond milk rava kesari is a fragrant South Indian dessert made with semolina (rava), ghee, sugar, water, and almonds. You can also add other nuts and dried fruits, such as cashews, raisins, and sultanas, to rava kesari.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. Fine semolina: 1/2 cup.
2. Homemade ghee: 3 tablespoons.
3. Powdered sugar: 2/3 cup.
4. Milk: 2 cups.
5. Homemade badam milk powder: 3 tablespoons.
6. Cardamoms: 2.
7. Saffron strands: 5.
8. Thinly sliced roasted almonds: 4.

Method Of Cocking

1. In a non-stick pan, roast whole green cardamom pods for 30 seconds, or until warm.
2. Peel and pound the cardamom pods in a mortar and pestle, then set aside.
3. Soak saffron in 2 teaspoons of warm milk.
4. Add about 1 tablespoon of ghee to the pan and roast sliced almonds until golden brown, then set aside.
5. Add rava to the pan and roast slowly over low heat for 2â€“3 minutes, or until aromatic, but not burnt.
6. Add milk to the pan and bring to a boil, then add badam milk powder, cardamom, and saffron strands.
7. Slowly add the roasted rava and whisk until it reaches a porridge-like consistency.
8. Add sugar to taste, about 3/4 to 1 cup, and stir continuously until it dissolves.
9. Continue cooking and stirring until the mixture thickens slightly.
