DAL KA DULHA

Fara, made from rice flour, is a much-loved dish in UP. Phere made from rice flour is also called desi momos. Rice flour contains choline, which helps in taking cholesterol and triglycerides from the liver into the body.

Method Of Cocking

1. Gather all the ingredients.
2. Take rice flour and cooked rice in a bowl.
3. Add salt as per taste and mix well.
4. Add a little water and knead it like dough.
5. Apply some oil and make the dough smooth.
6. Make small balls and give them long shapes.
7. Apply ghee in a plate and keep it aside.
8. Pour some water in a bowl and place a strainer over it.
9. Place the farra on the sieve and cover it with a plate.
10. Cook on low flame for 10 to 15 minutes.
11. Serve the prepared fara with spicy green chutney.
