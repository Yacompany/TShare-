BEASN CHEELA STUFF PANEER MASALA

Paneer stuffed besan chilla is a simple breakfast dish made with a besan batter spread into a thin chilla and stuffed with paneer and other ingredients.

5. ¾ tsp salt.
6. 1 cup water to make a smooth batter.

8. combine 1 cup paneer.
9. 1 carrot.
10. ½ capsicum.
11. 1 tomato.
12. 3 tbsp sweet corn.
13. 2 tbsp coriander.
14. ½ tsp salt.

Method Of Cocking

1. In a bowl, whisk together gram flour, red chili powder, carom seeds, baking soda, asafoetida, salt, chopped coriander, and enough water to make a semi-thick batter.
2. In a non-stick pan, heat oil and sauté onions and green chilies until translucent. Add red chili powder, paneer, chopped coriander, and salt, and mix well. Remove from heat and set aside.
3. Heat some oil in the same pan and pour a portion of the batter. Cook the base until completely cooked, then flip and cook the other side.
4. Spread 1 tsp of oil on the cooked chilla, cover, and roast on low heat until cooked through. Top with the paneer stuffing and cover with the chili. Serve immediately.
