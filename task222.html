BEETROOT LEMON DRISSING SALAD

Our superfood spotlight for April is a vegetable that is as vibrant in color as it is versatile: beetroots (commonly referred to as beets). These hardy root vegetables are known for their earthy, sweet taste and they boast an impressive nutrient profile.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

2. cucumber.
3. onions.
4. shredded carrot.
5. red or yellow bell pepper.
6. red onion.
7. radish.
8. fresh parsley.

2. 1 cup.

6. ¼ cup.
7. ¼ cup.
8. ¾ cup.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Scrub and trim beets then wrap in foil.
2. Place beets on baking sheet and bake until tender or for 1 ¼ – 1 ½ hours.
3. Peel beets and cut them into cubes.
4. Place the remaining vegetables and parsley in a large bowl. Whisk together dressing ingredients; toss with cucumber mixture. Gently stir in beets.
