PAAKNAM

Paaknam is a side dish quite popular in Manipur.It is of various kinds - besan paaknam,kanglayen paaknam,laphu tharo paaknam,nga marum paaknam,etc.It is basically a baked preparation.It is also a street food available in most street food stalls.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. Fish spawn.
2. Besan.
3. Napakpi(hooker chives).
4. Green chillies.
5. Ngari(fermented fish).
6. Salt.

Item Quantity

1. 150 gms.
2. 150 gm.
3. 3/4 bunches chopped.
4. 10-15.
5. 7/8 (steamed).
6. Salt - to taste.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. In a bowl,put the fish spawn,besan,napakpi,turmeric and mix well.Keep aside.
2. In another bowl,mash the green chillies,ngari and salt well,add a cup of water,stir and pour over the besan mixture.
3. Mix the besan mixture well adding enough water to form a stiff dough texture.keep aside.
4. Now lay out the plaintain leaves with the turmeric leaves on top and spread the dough on it.
5. Wrap it up into a square or rectangle shape,place it in the pressure cooker gently and let it whistle 3 times.
6. Release the pressure immediately and pan roast till the banana leaves becomes brownish on both sides.
7. Atlernatively, you can bake in the oven for 40/45 mins.
8. Remove the banana and turmeric leaves, cut into squares and serve.
