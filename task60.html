CHHA GOSHT

Chha Gosht is a traditional Pakistani dish. It is made from soft lamb meat cooked in a rich and flavourful gravy. Chha Gosht is a traditional recipe associated with Chamba in Himachal Pradesh.

Method Of Cocking

1. In a pressure cooker, add mutton, whole spices, and a little salt and pressure cook for 3-4 whistles or until the mutton is almost cooked.
2. Take out the mutton pieces in a plate and save some of the mutton stock.
3. Take the mutton pieces in a bowl, add ginger and garlic paste and crush them.
4. Heat oil in a pan on medium flame.
5. When hot, fry coriander powder, asafoetida and onion.
