CHAUWARAK

Chuwarak is a rice and jaggery distillate. The recipe varies, and thus it may contain jackfruit or pineapple. The colourless liquid has a pleasant flavour but is also extremely strong.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. Mishti Doi, chilled.
2. Chire, beaten rice.
3. Fully ripe sweet Mango, large.
4. Desiccated Coconut.
5. Cashew nuts.
6. Raisin.----------------

Item Quantity

2. 250g.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. Wash Chire under running water twice. Be careful while washing not to break grains.
-------------- 2. Soak raisin in a bowlful of water for 10 mins at room temperature.
3. Peel mangoes, discard seeds. Roughly chop 2 of them and make smooth puree with rest 2 of mangoes.
4. In two large bowls equally divide chilled Mishti Doi, Mango puree, washed Chire. Top with chopped mangoes, desiccated coconut, cashew and raisins.
5. You can add 1-2 tsp sugar in mango puree if mangoes are not sweet enough.
