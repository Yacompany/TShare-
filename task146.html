VEGETABLE PARATHA

Vegetable paratha is a traditional Indian flatbread made with wheat flour and fresh vegetables. It's a staple breakfast dish that's often eaten with curd, butter, and pickles.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. 1.5 cups wheat flour.
2. 1/4 cup shredded carrot.
3. 1/2 cup shredded cabbage.
4. 1/2 cup finely chopped capsicum.
5. 2 tablespoons finely chopped spring onions.
6. 2 tablespoons finely chopped fenugreek leaves.
7. 1/2 teaspoon ginger.
8. 1/2 teaspoon garlic.
9. 1/2 teaspoon powdered turmeric.
10. 1/4 cup boiled, mashed peas.

Method Of Cocking

1. In a bowl, combine 1 cup of wheat flour, 1 teaspoon of oil, and salt. Add chopped coriander leaves, green chili, red chili powder, garam masala powder, and coriander powder. Mix well, then gradually add water until the dough is smooth and knead. Cover and let the dough rest for 10 minutes.
2. Steam or boil a variety of vegetables until just cooked, then mash until smooth.
