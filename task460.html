BENGOLI MISHIT PULAO

Bengali Basanti Pulao also refer to as Mishti Pulao (Sweet Pilaf/ yellow rice) is a festive pleasure. It tastes pretty amazing on its own, but you should try it with Kosha Mangsho (Bengali Style Mutton Stew).

Method Of Cocking

1. Wash the rice throughly to remove all the cloudy water and excess starch.
2. Layer the washed rice on a colander or a place to air dry it for 15 minutes.
3. Transfer the rice to a bowl and to that add the turmeric and 2 tablespoons ghee. Give a mix to incorporate everything. Then cover and leave it aside for 1 hour.
4. After an hour start preparing the misti pulao. Bring a heavy bottom wok or skillet to medium heat.
5. Pour the remaining ghee along with cashew and raisins. Stir it around. Then add the bay leaves, clove and cardamoms. Toss it around for few seconds.
6. Scatter the marinated rice and toast it for 5 minutes.
7. Pour 1 and ½ cup hot water, mix it around and cover the pan. Let it cook in medium low heat for 10 minutes.
8. After 10 minutes, remove the lid. The rice should be cooked through and not mushy.
9. Using a fork fluff the rice.
10. Then sprinkle the sugar all around. Also add the green chili.
11. Fluff it one more time using the fork, cover the pan and leave it aside for 5 minutes.
12. Enjoy Basanti pulao fresh
