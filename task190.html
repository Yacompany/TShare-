Method Of Cocking

1. Soak urad dal, fenugreek seeds, and other ingredients in water for at least 2 hours.
2. Drain the soaked ingredients.
3. Blend the soaked ingredients with water until smooth.
4. Add flours, salt, and more water to the batter.
5. Cover and ferment the batter overnight in a warm place.
6. Mix the batter again after fermentation.
7. Add fruit salt and water to the batter before steaming.
8. Grease idli molds and pour spoonfuls of batter into them.
9. Steam the idlis for 10–12 minutes.
10. Serve with chutney or sambar.
