GAULASH (HUNGARY)

Goulash soup is a Hungarian dish of meat and vegetables in a paprika-seasoned broth. It's a national dish of Hungary and is commonly eaten in Central Europe and other parts of Europe.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. 2–3 lbs of potatoes.
2. 4.5 lbs of beef shank.
3. 5 large onions.
4. 3 large tomatoes.
5. 3 green peppers.
6. 1 large carrot.
7. 1 large root parsley.
8. 1/2 cup of lard or vegetable oil.
9. 1 lb of elbow macaroni.

Method Of Cocking

1. Cook the onion until the edges are light golden before adding the beef.
2. Don't brown the beef if there's too much in the pot, as the flavors will meld together later.
3. Scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot before combining the ingredients.
4. Easy goulash: This recipe uses ground beef, diced tomatoes, Worcestershire sauce, and cheddar cheese.
5. Beef goulash: This recipe uses stewing beef, white wine, and sour cream.
6. Spicy Hungarian goulash: This recipe uses cheaper cuts of beef and can be garnished with sour cream.
