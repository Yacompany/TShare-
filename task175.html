TANDOORI FISH TIKKA

Tandoori fish tikka is a popular, low-carb appetizer of boneless fish pieces coated in a thick tandoori marinade and grilled in a tandoor, a clay oven. The fish is cooked until crispy and has a smoky flavor from the fats dripping onto the hot coals while grilling.

Method Of Cocking

1. In a bowl, combine hung yogurt, chili powder, garam masala, salt, dried fenugreek powder, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and mustard oil to make a marinade.
2. Add fish cubes to the marinade and mix until well coated.
3. Marinate for 1–2 hours.
4. Preheat the oven to 200°C.
5. Skewer the fish cubes with other ingredients like onion, tomato, and capsicum.
6. Grease a baking tray with butter and place the skewers on top.
7. Bake for 20 minutes.
8. Baste the fish with butter every 10 minutes to make it crispy.
9. Serve with lemon, onion roundels, and chaat masala.
