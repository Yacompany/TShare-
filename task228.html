KOSAMBARI SALAD

Kosambari is a healthy, wholesome, vegan, and gluten-free salad that requires minimal ingredients. You can sample a variety of Kosambari salads in Maharashtrian, Kannada, or Tamil Cuisines.

Method Of Cocking

1. Rinse and soak yellow moong dal for 1 hour in water.
2. Cook it in salted water till al dente. Transfer to a colander and set aside to cool down.
4. Combine diced cucumbers, crushed peanuts, grated coconut, boiled moong dal, green chilli, salt, sugar, and spices. Gently toss them.
5. Heat oil in a small tadka pan. Add black mustard seeds, curry leaves, and once the seeds start popping, pour this tadka over the salad.
6. Add lemon juice, pomegranate, and chopped fresh coriander. Toss Kosambari Salad gently. It is ready to serve.
