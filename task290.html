GALAWATI KEBAB

Galouti kebab is a Lucknowi famous street food dish. It is made using mutton | lamb mince which is flavoured with spices rose extract, kewra water and other good stuffs. Galouti kebab has raw papaya paste added into the mixture to make the meat melt in mouth tender.

Method Of Cocking

1. Start by preparing the galouti masala. Dry roast all the spices on very low heat for 4 to 5 mins till toasted. Cool it down and grind it in to a fine powder. Set aside. This is your galouti masala.
2. Grind raw papaya with skins in a blender till it is smooth. Set aside.
3. Dry roast chana dal till toasted. Set aside in a bowl. If you don’t have chana dal, check notes for alternatives.
4. Take chana dal in a blender along with cashews or melon seeds and make it in a fine powder.
5. Add in the fried onions.
6. Add green chillies and make it into a paste.
7. Don’t add water. Set this aside till use.
8. Take minced lamb and lamb fat in a food processor or blender jar.
9. grind it till smooth and paste like.
10. Set aside in a bowl till needed.
11. Add raw papaya paste.
12. Add in red chilli and black pepper powder.
13. Add galouti special masala powder.
14. Add in ginger garlic paste.
15. Add coriander and mint leaves.
16. Add coriander and mint leaves.
17. add kewra water.
18. Add in fresh cream.
19. Season with salt.
