Method Of Cocking

1. Bring water to boil, in a pan Reduce heat and add the soda or fermented bean paste Add a little salt. The water will froth. Keep the pan on low heat and wait for the frothing to subside.
2. Add potato slices and cauliflower florets, stalks and leaves. Add the green chili Add the slices of beans and stir it all well. Add the drained rice and again stir.
3. Raise the heat and bring the stew to a boil. Reduce heat and allow to simmer, stirring frequently, for 15-20 minutes, until the rice is cooked. The vegetables should be tender and the water absorbed. Check for seasoning and add a little more salt if required. Remove from the heat and transfer to a serving dish. Serve hot.
