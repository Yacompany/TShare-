MUDHI MANSA

Mudhi mansa is 1of the famous snack dish of Odisha. It is mostly famous in "Baripada" a town situated in the Dist-Mayurbhanj of Odisha, Mudhi mansa means Mutton alu curry (mansa)is eaten with Mudhi or puffed rice, dis snack dish is sufficient to fill up your appetite, I have taken chicken here for the recipe,so let's go for it.

Method Of Cocking

1. Heat oil in pan, add chopped onions and fry till it turns brown, make a paste of ginger garlic and add to it and fry for 2 mins then add turmeric, green chilli, chilli powder, chopped tomato, cumin and Coriander powder and stir still it leaves oil.
2. Add chicken, fry for 5 mins then add potato,salt to taste, garam masala and fry in Medium flame till it becomes dry.
3. Now add water accordingly and cook till the consistency becomes thick, at last add chicken masala, leave for 1min, add chopped coriander, mix and turn out the flame.
4. Now your mansa or chicken curry is ready to be served with mudhi or puffed rice, just try once, it's a finger licking snack dish, thank you.
