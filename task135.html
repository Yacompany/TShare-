ALOO SANDWICH

An aloo sandwich typically makes four servings, and uses eight slices of bread. Aloo sandwiches can be made with a variety of ingredients, including potatoes, onions, tomatoes, and spices.

2. six slices of bread.

4. one tablespoon of green chutney.
5. three or four boiled potatoes.
6. quarter teaspoon of asafoetida.
7. one medium onion.
8. two to three medium boiled and mashed potatoes.
9. one or two green chilies.
10. one medium tomato.

Method Of Cocking

1. Prepare the potato filling: Mash potatoes and mix with finely chopped onions, coriander, green chilies, chili powder, chaat masala, lemon juice, and salt.
2. Spread condiments: Spread butter on both slices of bread, and green chutney on one slice. You can also spread tomato sauce on one slice.
3. Add filling: Spread a generous amount of potato filling on the buttered slice with chutney.
4. Top: Top with grated cheese.
5. Close: Cover with the other slice of bread and press gently.
6. Toast: Place the sandwich in a cast-iron skillet or nonstick tawa over medium heat, buttered side down. Toast for 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown. Flip and toast the other side until golden brown.
7. Serve: Cut the sandwich in half and serve hot.
