FRUIT CUSTARD SALAD

Fruit custard is one of those desserts that reminds me of childhood. Growing up, we would eat Carrot Halwa, Rice Kheer, Besan Ladoo or a fruit custard like this one for our sweet treat.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. whole milk.
2. sugar.
3. custard powder.
4. milk warm or at room temperature.
5. apples, mangoes, sapota, banana, pears, strawberries, pomegranate.

Item Quantity

1. 2.5 cups.
2. 5 tablespoons.
3. 3 tablespoons.
4. 3 tablespoons .
5. 1.5 to 2 cups.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. Take 2.5 cups milk in a thick bottomed pan or saucepan and keep it on the stove top. Bring the milk to a gentle simmer.
2. While the milk is warming up, take 3 tablespoons warm or room temperature milk in a small bowl. Add 3 tablespoons custard powder.
3. You can take the 3 tablespoons warm milk from the milk which is getting heated up or warm 3 tablespoons milk separately in a microwave oven or on the stove top.
4. Or swap custard powder with 3 tablespoons of cornstarch. Don’t forget to add 1 teaspoon vanilla extract or ½ teaspoon of vanilla essence to this mix.
