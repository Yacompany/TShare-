CHOWDER (USA)

Chowder is a thick soup or stew made with milk or cream, a roux, and seafood or vegetables. It's usually made with salt pork, onions, and potatoes, and can also include other vegetables.

Method Of Cocking

1. Wash and chop vegetables.
2. Add vegetables to a pan with water, salt, and pepper.
3. Bring to a boil and simmer for about 15 minutes.
4. Prepare a white sauce with butter, flour, and milk.
5. Add the white sauce to the vegetables and mixture.
6. Add cheese and paprika and stir until the cheese melts.
