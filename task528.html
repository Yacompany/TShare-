ROSEN AON

Rosep Aon is a traditional recipe and the signature dish of the Ao Naga Tribe of Nagaland. It is a dish made of mixed vegetables, dry fish and bamboo shoots. Rosep means dry and Aon means dish.

Method Of Cocking

1. In a mortar and pestle coarsely ground the green chillies, ginger and garlic.
2. Chop all the vegetables and add them in a large pot.
3. Add the coarsely grounded green chillies, ginger, garlic, tomatoes, dry fish, bamboo shoots juice and salt.
4. Mix them well together nicely. Add enough water covering the ingredients fully.
5. Cover the pan and cook on medium.
6. Cook till the water is evaporated completely and the curry is dry. Please do not stir while cooking this dish. Adjust the salt before the water dries up.
7. Take off the heat and remove the cover of the pot and let it cool down completely. This dish is serve after completely getting cooled down. Taste even better when served the next day.
