AAM PAPAD

a traditional and authentic sun-dried mango pulp recipe served as confectionery or condiments. the recipe is very simple and easy to make as it does not contain any complicated ingredients.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. mango.
2. sugar.
3. cardamom powder.
4. ghee / clarified butter.

Item Quantity

1. 2 cup.
2. 2 tbsp.
3. ¼ tsp.
4. ½ tsp.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Kinfe
Kadhai
Medium Size Handi
Choppring Board
Foil Pack
Ladle
Palta
Bhatti
Baking Tray

Method Of Cocking

1. firstly, in the blender take 2 cup mango and 2 tbsp sugar.
2. blend to smooth paste without adding any water.
3. transfer the mango puree to the kadai and begin to cook.
4. stir continuously and cook on medium flame until it thickens well.
5. now add ¼ tsp cardamom powder and mix well.
6. further, brush ½ tsp ghee on a steel plate.
7. transfer the puree onto the plate and spread uniformly.
8. sundry for 2 days or a week, until it turns translucent.
9. scrape the sides using the knife. peel gently without damaging the aam papad.
10. finally, cut into the desired shape and roll AAM PAPAD.
