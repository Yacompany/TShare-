KACHALU CHAAT

Kachalu chaat, also known as kachaloo chaat, is a popular Indian street food made with kachalu, a root vegetable with a mild, nutty taste and starchy texture. Here's a recipe for kachalu chaat.

Method Of Cocking

1. Wash and boil 2 kachalu.
2. Let them cool, then peel and cut into small pieces.
3. Add red chili powder, salt, roasted jeera powder, chaat masala, and black pepper powder.
4. Add 2 finely chopped green chilies.
5. Add coriander leaves, tamarind pulp, and lemon juice.
6. Mix well with a spatula, then cover and let sit for 1â€“2 hours.
7. Garnish with coriander and squeeze lemon juice on top.
