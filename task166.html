Method Of Cocking

1. Dissolve alum in a liter of water and set aside.
2. Dissolve kitchen lime in a liter of water and strain through a fine sieve or cloth.
3. Wash, dry, peel, and cut the ash gourd in half.
4. Discard the pith and seeds, then cut into 2-inch pieces.
5. Prick each piece of ash gourd with a fork.
6. Soak the ash gourd pieces in the lime water for 4–5 hours.
7. Drain and rinse the pieces thoroughly with water.
8. Boil the ash gourd pieces in the alum water for 20 minutes until translucent.
9. Heat sugar and water in a heavy-bottomed pan until the water boils and the sugar dissolves.
10. Add the ash gourd pieces, crushed saffron, and salt.
11. Simmer, stirring occasionally, for 6–7 minutes.
12. Turn off the heat and let the pieces soak in the syrup for 9–10 hours.
13. Reheat the mixture, stir, and boil for 4–5 minutes until the sugar reaches a syrupy consistency.
14. Turn off the heat and let the pieces soak in the syrup for another 10–12 hours.
