PHO (VIETNAM)

Pho is a Vietnamese soup made with broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat. The word "pho" technically refers to the noodles, but the dish is often considered a single unit.Pho is Vietnam's national dish and a popular breakfast food in Hanoi.

Method Of Cocking

1. Rinse the poha in a strainer under running water until it's soft. You can also sprinkle some water over the poha and let it soak, but don't over-rinse or soak it, or it will get mushy.
2. Add turmeric and salt to the poha and mix.
3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat, then add mustard seeds and let them pop.
4. Add peanuts and sauté until they start to brown.
5. Add chopped onion, green chili, and curry leaves and cook until the onions soften.
6. Add washed rice to the pan and mix well.
7. Cook for 2–3 minutes over medium heat.
8. Serve hot.
