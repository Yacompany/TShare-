MEWA LADOO

Mewa ladoo is a dry fruit-based Indian sweet that is often eaten during festivals, family functions, and other occasions. The word "mewa" means "nuts" and "panch" means "five," so the ladoo is made with five different dry fruits or nuts.

Method Of Cocking

1. Dry roast almonds and cashews in a pan or microwave until aromatic.
2. Add pistachios and fry for a couple of minutes.
3. Remove the nuts from heat and let them cool.
4. Add dried fruits like dates, apricots, and figs to the pan and stir until they are slightly warm.
5. Toast any seeds until they are aromatic.
6. Grind the nuts into a mush.
7. Add cardamom powder to the nuts and blend until well combined.
8. Apply ghee to your palms and shape the mixture into small ladoos.
