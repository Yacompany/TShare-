MAROI THONGBA

Maroi thongba is a manipuri main dish,a must served dish in any vegetarian feast.It is a vegetarian preparation,the main ingredient being maroi nakuppi,a locally available herb.

Method Of Cocking

1. Heat the oil till smoking hot in a pressure cooker.
2. Now fry the peanuts till it crackles on medium heat.Take out on a plate and keep aside.
3. Next in the same oil,lightly fry the barhi/boris and keep aside.
4. Now put the bay leaves,sliced onions,ginger garlic paste and cook till the onion turns golden brown.
5. Add whole jeera,the grounded spices.
6. Next put maroi nakuppi,potatoes,thanging(optional),the fried peanuts and boris,stir and cook for 2 mins.
7. Now add turmeric,chilli powder,salt and 1 cup water.
8. Stir and close the lid of the cooker and let it whistle 5 times.
9. Release the pressure immediately,take out the lid and cook for a minute.
10. Garnish with the chopped coriander and served.
