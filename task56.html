LASSI

Lassi is a yogurt-based drink that originated in Punjab, India, with a smoothie-like consistency. It's made by blending yogurt with water and various fruits or seasonings, such as salt or sugar. Lassi can be made sweet or salty.

Method Of Cocking

1. Use full fat yogurt for a thick and creamy drink.
2. Use chilled water to bring the yogurt to consistency.
3. Add mint leaves, jeera powder, lemon juice, and salt to beat the summer heat.
4. Pour the lassi into clay glasses for an authentic feel.
5. Whisk plain yogurt in a bowl until smooth.
6. Add cumin powder and black salt, and mix well.
7. Add water to thin out the lassi. For a thicker lassi, add 1/2 cup water, and for a thinner lassi, add 1 cup or more.
8. Pour into a glass, garnish with mint leaves, and sprinkle with roasted cumin powder.
