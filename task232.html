ROASTED SWEET POTATO SALAD

Every time I roast sweet potatoes, I wonder why I don’t make it more often. So juicy and soft on the inside, naturally sweet, and caramelises beautifully on the outside. I can’t think of another vegetable that caramelises this well.

Method Of Cocking

1. Preheat oven to 220C / 430F.
2. Cut sweet potato into 1.5cm / 3/5" thick slices. Cut the larger rounds in half (into semi circles).
4. Place in a bowl, drizzle over oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper. Toss well to coat. Pour onto baking tray.
5. Roast for 20 minutes, then use an egg flip to turn and roast for a further 10 minutes or until golden.
6. Meanwhile, place Dressing ingredients in a jar, mix with a teaspoon (to get honey off the base) then shake well until combined.
7. Toast pecans in a dry skillet over medium high heat for 3 minutes, or until they smell nutty.
8. Remove pecans, then heat 1 tsp oil. Add bacon and cook until golden.
9. To assemble, place everything in a bowl, reserving some bacon, pecans and goats cheese for garnish. Drizzle with most of the dressing, then toss gently.
10. Transfer into a serving bowl, garnish with remaining bacon, pecans and goats cheese. Drizzle with remaining dressing. Serve.
