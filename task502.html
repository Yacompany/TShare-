ALOO POTALA RASA

This is so yummilicious pointed gourd curry/ gravy recipe. This is a traditional recipe of Odisha. In this curry pointed guards are cooked along with potatoes in a coconut and cashew nut gravy.

Method Of Cocking

1. Prepare the masala paste.
2. Prepare the white paste.
3. Cut the pointed guards and potatoes.
4. Heat a pan and add 4 tsp oil in it and then put the pointed gourds in it.
5. Then add half of the salt, turmeric powder and chilli powder in it and saute it till golden brown by covering the lid.
6. Then remove them from the pan and add the potatoes in it and also saute it well for coating of the masala.
7. Keep both of them aside.
8. Then in the same pan put oil in it and then add sugar. When it becomes caramelize then add the red chilli and bay leaves in it.
9. Then add the chopped onion in it and saute it well till it becomes golden brown in colour.
10. Then add the masala paste in it.
