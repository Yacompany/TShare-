MALAI FRUIT'S SANDWICH

Malai sandwiches are a popular Indian street food that can be made with a variety of fillings, bread, and clotted cream (malai). The bread is spread with malai and then filled with a mixture of vegetables, cheese, and spices, and then grilled or toasted until golden and crispy.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. In a large bowl.
2. combine ½ carrot.
3. 3 tbsp cabbage.
4. ½ capsicum.
5. ½ onion.
6. 3 tbsp sweet corn.
7. 2 tbsp coriander.
8. Add ½ cup cream.
9. ½ tsp pepper powder.
10. ½ tsp chilli flakes.
11. ½ tsp mixed herbs.
12. Spread 2 tbsp of the vegetable malai mixture on a bread slice.

Method Of Cocking

1. Wash chhena to remove vinegar flavor and squeeze out extra water.
2. Hang the tied cloth for 30 minutes.
3. Grind the chhena until smooth.
4. Mash the chhena with your palm and add flour.
5. Mix and mash for 10 minutes until smooth.
6. Form a small ball to check for cracks.
7. Divide the chhena into equal balls and roll into desired shapes.
8. Make a vegetable mixture with onion, capsicum, carrot, green chilies, salt, crushed black peppercorns, and fresh malai.
9. Spread butter and green chutney on four slices of white bread.
10. Spread the vegetable mixture on each slice.
11. Sprinkle grated cheese on top.
12. Cover with the remaining bread slices.
13. Spread butter on top.
14. Grill until golden brown on both sides.
15. Cut each sandwich into two triangles.
16. Serve hot with tomato ketchup and green chutney.
