WAHAN MOSODENG

A pork salad of sorts, the preparation of Wahan Mosdeng involves marinating the meat with a mixture of spices and herbs, including garlic, ginger, turmeric, and mustard oil. The marinated meat is then grilled on a charcoal fire until it is tender and juicy.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. Pork, Cut into pieces.
2. Onion, thinly sliced .
3. Green chillies ( Adjust according to taste bud ) .
4. Tbsp Ginger, finely chopped .
5. Coriander leaves, finely chopped .
6. Salt to taste.

Item Quantity

1. 250 gms.
2. 1 Big.
3. 5-6 Green.
4. 1 1/2 Tbsp.
5. 1/4 Cup.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. Wash the pork pieces and pressure cook it for 3 whistles by adding little water.
2. Open the lid and cook it for another 2 minutes or until the water gets absorbed. This helps to keep the flavour in pork.
3. Once done, cut in small pieces and keep aside.
4. Dry roast the green chillies and grind it without adding water. Keep aside.
5. Take the chopped pork pieces in a bowl and add the ground chilli followed by thinly sliced onion, ginger, coriander leaves and salt.
6. Mix well and serve.
