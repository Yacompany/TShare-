MIX FRUIT CHUTNEY

Mix fruit chutney- Pickles and chutneys or relishes are Indian style accompaniments to serve along with the main course or to serve with snacks. Some are made daily and some are made in big batches as they have a good shelf life.

Method Of Cocking

1. Peel,deseed and chop orange into small pieces.
2. Peel,core and chop apples into small pieces.
3. Peel and chop kiwi ,peach ,raw mango and plum.
4. Heat oil in a heavy bottom pan and add cumin and when it start to crackle add cardamom and cinnamon.
5. Now add all the chopped fruits and 3 tbsps of water.
6. Cover and cook on low heat till apples become soft .
7. Now add sugar,ginger and all the spices and simmer till it start to thicken.
8. Let it cool down completely and then store in a glass bottle
9. Keep refrigerated .
