Method Of Cocking

1. Take 1 cup idli rice or parboiled rice (ukda chawal, sela chawal) in a bowl.
2. Rinse the rice grains a couple of times. Then, add 1 to 1.5 cups water and keep aside.
3. Rinse ¼ cup thick poha (flattened rice) once or twice and add to the rice. Mix well. Cover and keep aside for 4 to 5 hours.
4. In another bowl, take ½ cup urad dal and ¼ teaspoon fenugreek seeds.
5. Rinse for a couple of times and soak the urad dal and fenugreek seeds together in 1 cup water for 4 to 5 hours.
6. After 4 to 5 hours, drain the urad dal-fenugreek seeds and add in a grinder jar or a sturdy blender.
7. Grind the urad dal and fenugreek seeds till you get a smooth and fluffy batter. Add water in parts while grinding.
8. Next, drain the idli rice and poha. Add them to the same grinder jar. Depending on your jar capacity, you can grind these in 2 to 3 batches.
9. Grind the rice-poha till smooth or a fine granular consistency in the batter is achieved.
