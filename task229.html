KACHUMBAR SALAD

Sweet-tangy tomatoes, crunchy cucumbers and piquant onions form the basis of this simple Kachumber salad recipe that goes well with almost any Indian dish.

Method Of Cocking

1. In a mixing bowl, add ⅓ cup chopped onions, ½ cup chopped tomatoes and ½ cup chopped cucumbers.
2. Add ¼ cup chopped coriander (a.k.a. fresh cilantro) leaves, 1 tablespoon chopped mint leaves and ½ to 1 teaspoon chopped green chillies (approximately 1 small green chili).
4. Add ¼ teaspoon red chili powder, cayenne or sweet paprika, ½ teaspoon roasted cumin powder or ground cumin and 2 to 3 pinches black salt (optional). Season with regular table salt or pink salt as needed.
5. Add 2 teaspoons lemon juice. I find 2 teaspoons of lemon juice perfect for my taste, but you can add less or more of it.
