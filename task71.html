THIKUA

Thekua is a traditional, deep-fried cookie from the Indian subcontinent that's popular in Bihar and Uttar Pradesh, and in the Terai region of Nepal. It's also known as Khajuria, Tikari, and Thokni.

Method Of Cocking

1. To make thekua softer, knead the dough with more ghee.
2. You can add some saffron milk to make it more flavorful.
3. The mixture should be warm or cool enough to handle.
4. When you press the mixture, it should form a lump and not fall apart.
5. You can add ½ cup chopped jaggery with ¼ cup water in a small saucepan, and heat until all the jaggery melts on medium-low heat.
