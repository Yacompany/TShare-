MOONGLET SWEET CHUTNEY

Moonglet's instant moringa leaves chutney is a powder mix that can be used to make 300 grams of fresh chutney by adding twice as much water. It's made with fresh, seasonal ingredients and contains moderate amounts of salt, sugar, and chili.
