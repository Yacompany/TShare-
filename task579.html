CHAGEMPOMBA

Chagempomba is a dish that the manipuris enjoy eating but it is a dish of acquired taste.Anybody who doesn't have much knowledge or acquaintance with the manipuri cuisine may be repulsed by just the sight or the smell of this particular dish BUT once one acquires the taste,it will surely be a dish that cannot be forgotten easily.

Method Of Cocking

1. In a big bowl,put chopped hangam,mangra angangba mana,hawaimaton,rice,1 tbsp oil and mash well so that the vegetables become tender and transfer into a pressure cooker.Add ekai thabi,yongchak ,5 or 6 cups water,salt,turmeric,red chillies,ginger and let it whistle 2 times.Alternative ly, the ingredients can be cooked in an open big pot till it reaches kheer like consistency.
2. Release the pressure immediately and stir well till it becomes soggy.Add khajing,nga ayaiba and simmer for 5 mins. or it attains kheer like consistency.
3. Now add hawaijar,pakhon, mix well and simmer.
4. In a wok,heat 4 tbsp oil till smoking and add bay leaves,nakuppi,onion and cook till onion becomes golden brown,then add maru marang ,stir once and pour into the cooker and simmer for a minute.Remove from heat and serve.
