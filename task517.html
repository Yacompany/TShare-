SMOKED PORK STEW

This Pulled Pork Stew dish is well worth the wait. It’s low and slow, just like we smoke our SADLER’S SMOKEHOUSE® Pit Smoked & Seasoned Pulled Pork. Perfection takes time, after all… try this dish today and find out for yourself.

Method Of Cocking

1. In a stock pot over medium heat, combine all ingredients except pork. Cover and bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 to 1 ½ hours until red potatoes and carrots are tender.
3. Add pulled pork and continue simmering for 20 minutes.
4. Serve stew over hot rice.
