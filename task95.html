MOONG DAAL PALAK CHEELA

Moong Dal Palak Cheela can also be served with tomato sauce or green coriander chutney. The cheela batter should neither be too thin nor too thick. It is better to use non-stick pan to make cheela.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. 3/4 cup yellow moong dal (split yellow gram).
2. 1/2 cup finely chopped spinach (palak).
3. 1 tsp green chili paste.
4. 1/2 tsp ginger (adrak) paste.
5. 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing).
6. 1/4 tsp chilli powder.
7. 1/4 tsp turmeric powder (haldi).
8. 1/2 tsp coarsely crushed cumin seeds (jeera).
10. Salt to taste.

Method Of Cocking

1. Soak yellow moong dal in water for two hours.
2. Drain the dal and blend it with half a cup of water until smooth.
3. Add the remaining ingredients to the batter, along with about a quarter cup of water, and mix well.
4. Heat a non-stick griddle and grease it with a little oil.
5. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the griddle and spread it into a 7-inch circle.
6. Cook for one minute over medium heat.
7. Brush the chilla with a little oil, and along the edges.
8. Cook until the base is light golden brown and the top is dry.
9. Serve hot with chutney, pickle, or sauce.
