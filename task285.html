GOSHT KHICDHI

Khichda is a simple one-pot dish made with whole wheat, lentils, mutton pieces and spices. The dish gives us the same comfort like a khichdi does, only with much more flavours in it.

Method Of Cocking

1. Mix the rice and all the dals and then cook it in 3 cups of water and salt to taste till tender.
2. Heat oil, add cloves, peppercorns, cumin, cinnamon and saute till they change color a bit.
3. Add the onions, mutton, chilli powder, coriander powder, garam masala, salt and green paste.
4. Stir fry till meat is opaque, then cook in a pressure cooker till meat is tender.
5. Combine the meat, wheat and dal mixture, simmer for 2-3 minutes and serve garnished.
