Method Of Cocking

1. Dry roast the coconut and poppy seeds in 1 tsp oil in a pan. Once done, let it cool and grind it to a fine paste with a little water.
2. Marinate the prawns with salt and turmeric powder and set it aside.
3. In a pan, heat oil and fry the marinated prawns for about five minutes. Set them aside.
4. In another pan, heat some oil and fry the onion and ginger garlic paste till the raw smell disappears.
5. Next, add the xacuti paste and fry for a few minutes.
6. Season this with the rest of the spices and fry for a few more minutes. Add some water if necessary.
7. Add the fried prawns to this mixture. Taste the salt and add more if needed.
8. Throw in some more water into this. Cover and cook for about 10 minutes till everything comes together.
9. Switch off the gas and garnish this with fresh coriander leaves.
