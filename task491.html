MACHA GHANTA

Fish dishes being very common in Odisha, are relished by each unit and what higher than every odiya's favorite, Macha Ghanta! a requirement supply to Hindu deity Maa on Dussehra, this tempting curry consists of the cooked head of the fish and is served with hot steamed rice and dish.

Method Of Cocking

1. Grind the onion, garlic and ginger into a fine paste.
2. Grind cumin seeds, argemone, chillies and bay leave separately.
3. Fry the fishes in oil and keep it aside.
4. Cut the potatoes into small pieces after taking out the skin.
5. Fry the rice in oil. When it turns brown add potatoes and fry.
6. After 5 minutes add onion, ginger and garlic paste to it.
7. After frying a little add the cumin seed, argemone and chilli paste to it.
8. When nice flavour comes out from the spices pour 3 cups water. Let it boil for sometime.
9.
Add the fish to it and cover with lid. When it is cooked add the cardamom and cinnamon powder and remove from flame.
