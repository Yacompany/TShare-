Method Of Cocking

1. Fry onion, ginger, garlic, tomato and ground spices.
2. Boil this onion-tomato-spice mixture.
3. Cook the marinated fish on the pan.
4. Cook the prepared curry for some time.
5. When reheating the fish curry, do so carefully and monitor the curry until it comes to a boil.
6. Excessive boiling will make the fish tough. Heat the curry in a saucepan or wok over medium heat until completely hot.
7. Then remove the pan from heat or reduce the flame.
