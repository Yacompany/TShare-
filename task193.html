KUCHUMBARI SALAD

Kachumber is a fresh, crunchy, and flavorful Indian salad that's made without dressing and typically contains cucumbers, tomatoes, onions, cilantro or mint, and spices.

Method Of Cocking

1. Use fresh ingredients.
2. Add some crunch, color, and protein.
3. Mix sweet and savory flavors.
4. Add herbs.
5. To reduce the onion flavor, soak the chopped onion in water for 15 minutes before using.
6. You can also add other vegetables like avocado, pomegranate, or sweet corn.
7. If serving to kids, you can skip the green chili.
