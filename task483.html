ERACHI VARUTHARACHA CURRY

A spicy Kerala style beef curry.It is a type of malabar meat curry roasted in coconut paste.It is one of the most famous traditional dishes in Kerala.This recipe is simple,delicious and spicy in taste.This recipe is easy to made.

Method Of Cocking

1. Cut and wash the beef properly.
2. Marinate the beef with 1tsp kashmiri red chili powder,¼ tsp turmeric powder,½ tsp garam masala, 1tsp ground peppercorns.
3. Heat 1tbsp coconut oil in a frying pan , add shredded coconut, 1tsp fennel seeds, curry leaves, 1tbsp shallots and ground it.
4. Add the grounded mixed spices with the marinated beef in a pressure cooker and cook it until 3 whistles on low medium.
5. Heat 1tbsp oil in a cooking vessel, add 2 cups chopped onions and shallots, 2 green chilies, curry leaves and fry it for 2 mins.
6. Add 1tsp crushed ginger and garlic , fry till it gets golden.
