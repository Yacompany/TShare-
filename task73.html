BIYA KI SABJI

Biya Bhaji is a popular and spicy vegetarian dish of Goa. It is also called cashew curry or cashew tonak. It is usually prepared with fresh coconut and spices and served with roti, rice, or bread. Biya Bhaji is served at weddings and other special occasions in Goa.

Method Of Cocking

1. Marinate the chicken with salt, turmeric powder, and lemon juice.
2. Marinate the chicken with Kafrel masala and keep aside for 1-2 hours..
3. Heat oil in a pan and fry the marinated chicken pieces evenly on both sides for 10-15 minutes.
4. Once the chicken is cooked well, add 1 chopped onion and the remaining Kaffrel masala and some water.
5. Cook on low-medium flame for 10-15 minutes and serve with hot Goa Pav.
