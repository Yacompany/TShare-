VEGETABLE DALIYA

Vegetable dalia is a healthy, tasty, and mildly spicy Indian dish made with broken wheat, also known as cracked wheat, bulgur wheat, or couscous, and vegetables.

Method Of Cocking

1. Roast dalia: Heat ghee or oil in a pan and roast dalia for 2–3 minutes until aromatic.
2. Sauté: Heat oil or ghee in a pressure cooker and add cumin seeds, ginger, green chilies, and onion. Sauté until the onion is translucent and the ginger's raw aroma goes away.
3. Add vegetables: Sauté chopped tomatoes, potatoes, carrots, and peas for a couple of minutes.
4. Add remaining ingredients: Add moong dal, turmeric, and salt to taste. Sauté for a couple of minutes.
5. Pressure cook: Add water and pressure cook for 2–3 whistles.
6. Finish: After pressure drops, stir in butter, sambar powder, and chopped coriander and cook for a couple of minutes.
