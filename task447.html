Gahat ke Parantha is a popular winter snack in the hilly areas . It is eaten with fresh homemade butter and a little jaggery. The recipe for Gahat ke Parantha is as follows.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

2. whole wheat flour.
3. turmeric powder.
4. red chilli powder.
5. green chillies chopped.
6. ginger grated.

Method Of Cocking

1. Clean & soak the lentils for 4 hours.
2. Pressure cook it for 4-5 whistles so that the lentils cook completely.
3. Drain the cooked lentils & grind into a paste.
4. Now mix salt, turmeric & red chilli powder with the paste & mix well. Add chopped green chillies, grated ginger & curry leaves. Keep aside the stuffing.
5. Now with 1 cup of wheat flour, add a little salt, remaining turmeric & red chilli powder.
6. Make normal chapati dough. Take a small portion of dough & make a patty. Stuff some prepared lentil mixture & seal nicely.
7. Make a thick roti by rolling with a light hand.
8. Heat a skillet & roast the chapati on low flame so that it cooks thoroughly. Apply some oil/ghee.
9. Enjoy with any side dish or simply with bhang/til ki chutney.
