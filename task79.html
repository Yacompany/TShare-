ARBI KE KOFTA KA SALAN

Arbi ke kofta ka salan is a different type of salan from the common salan, which is made by mixing it with tomato and making it sour with tamarind.

Method Of Cocking

1. Wash the arbi thoroughly and boil it.
2. Peel the colocasia and mash it with a masher.
3. Peel the boiled potatoes, mash them and mix them in it.
4. Add corn flour, cumin powder, red chilli powder and salt as per taste to the mashed arbi and mix well.
5. Shape the kofta into desired shape.
6. To make gravy, heat cooking oil.
7. Add onion-tomato and spice paste and fry until its rawness goes away.
8. Add all the ground spices mentioned and fry on low flame.
