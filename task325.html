BHEJA FRY

Bheja Fry is one of the classics that are enjoyed widely all over the country. Made with lamb or sheep brain, this dish is also a delicacy in many places. The best part about this dish is that it is cooked with minimal ingredients but tastes heavenly.

Method Of Cocking

1. Wash the brain with normal water first and then boil it in 1 cup of water and add turmeric and 1 pinch of salt in it.
2. Heat some oil in a pan and add in the finely chopped onions to it. Once the onions cook and turn golden brown, add in the masalas and stir everything well. Add 2 tsp of water and mix everything together.
3. Now add in the boiled brain and mix well. Break it into bite-sized pieces and mix gently. Cook until the oil separates. Take it out in a serving bowl and serve with some hot naan or tandoori roti.
