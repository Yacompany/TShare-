BEDMI PURI SABJI

Bedmi Puri and Aloo Sabzi is a traditional Indian dish. Bedmi Puri is a flatbread made from whole wheat flour, urad dal, and spices. It is usually served with roasted potatoes or potato curry.

Method Of Cocking

1. Start kneading the dough by adding hot water.
2. Let the dough rest for 20 minutes so that the semolina absorbs the water.
3. Knead the dough for a minute and take a ball sized dough.
4. Roll a little thickly and place a ball sized stuffing in the middle.
5. Bring everything together and seal well.
6. slightly flat.
