DAL KA DULHA

Dal ka dulha, also known as dal pithi or dal pithori, is a dish from Bihari cuisine that's popular in Uttar Pradesh and Bihar. The dish is made with whole-wheat dumplings that are stuffed with arhar dal and then dipped in spicy lentils.

Method Of Cocking

1. Wash the lentils, boil them with turmeric and salt in the cooker for 10 minutes.
2. Knead the dough and take the dough and roll it into roti.
3. Cut out rounds from the bowl and give desired shape to make dal groom.
4. Add dal dal to boiled dal and boil for 10-15 minutes.
5. Heat oil in a pan, crackle asafoetida and cumin seeds and add all the ingredients and fry.
6. After frying well, add chopped tomatoes and powdered spices.
7. Once the spices are roasted well, add the dal dal and boil till it comes to a boil.
