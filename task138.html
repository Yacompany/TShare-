VEGETABLE NUTS UTTAPAM

Vegetable uttapam is a dish made with a batter and a variety of vegetables, and sometimes nuts.Uttapam is high in fiber and low in calories, and it contains vitamins, minerals, protein, and heart-healthy fats.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. 1 cup dosa batter.
2. 1 cup grated mixed vegetables, such as carrots, cabbage, cauliflower, and onions.
3. 1 tsp roasted peanuts powder.
4. 2 tbsp chopped coriander.
5. ½ tsp sugar.
6. Chili powder to taste.
7. Salt to taste.
8. 1 tsp sesame seeds.
9. Minimal oil.

Method Of Cocking

1. Bring idli or dosa batter to room temperature.
2. Heat a non-stick griddle or tawa over medium-high heat.
3. Grease the tawa with 1/4 teaspoon of oil.
4. Pour a ladleful of batter into the center of the tawa.
5. Sprinkle with finely chopped or grated vegetables and roasted nuts.
6. Drizzle with a few drops of oil.
7. Cover and cook for 2–3 minutes, or until small bubbles appear on the batter.
8. Flip the uttapam and cook until golden brown on the other side.
9. Serve hot with chutney.
