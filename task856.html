MIRCH KE TIPORE

Ker Sangri is a traditional Rajasthani dish made with dried ker berries and sangri beans. Here is a tried-and-tested authentic recipe for making it at home.

Method Of Cocking

1. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
2. Once the oil is hot, add asafetida, cumin seeds, and fennel seeds and saute for 3-4 seconds.
3. Add dry red chilies and fry for 4-5 seconds.
4. Now add coriander powder, chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, and dry mango powder and saute for 10-12 seconds.
5. Add the cooked ker and sangri and mix well.
6. Add yogurt, raisins, and 1 teaspoon salt and mix well.
7. Reduce the heat to low.
8. Cover the pan with a lid and cook for 6-8 minutes.
9. Check for salt and add more if needed.
10. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot.
