BOTI KEBAB

If you want to make restaurant style Boti Kebab at home, you are in the right place. This quick and easy Boti Kebab recipe will help you treat your family and friends with the best ever mutton kebabs. Soft, spicy and so flavourful, these boti kebab or mutton kebab would vanish from the plate as soon as served.

Method Of Cocking

1. To prepare this delicious kebab recipe, marinate the mutton cubes with garlic paste, papaya paste and green chilli paste. Sprinkle a little garam masala powder along with chilli powder over the mutton cubes and mix well. Put this aside for half an hour and let the mutton pieces marinate.
2. Add one cup of water to the pressure cooker and put it up on the high flame. Bring the water to a boil and add these marinated mutton cubes into the boiling water, cook until the water evaporates and the mutton pieces are tender and dry.
3. Grill the cooked mutton cubes in a preheated grill. Keep greasing the mutton cubes with butter frequently until smoking hot. Serve hot in a plate with mint chutney topped with onion rings.
