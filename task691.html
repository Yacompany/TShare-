Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. Sieve the flour and place it in a big bowl. Add a pinch of salt.
2. Heat 1 cup of water till the water starts to simmer. Take off and mix with the flour. Depending on the quality of the flour the quantity will vary. Start with less water and gradually add more to get the right consistency.
3. Mix to get a lump-free batter and keep it pourable yet thick like a slightly thinner pancake batter. Keep it aside for 5-7 minutes till it cools down a bit then add the egg and mix.
4. Now adjust the consistency with room temperature water. The thickness should be enough to pour and spread easily by swirling the pan. Please check the associated video.
