SAKI NALU

Sakinalu are a very popular and a traditional snack from Telangana, India. Sakinalu are prepared with a special, freshly ground rice flour and sesame seeds. Rice is soaked for a few hours and then ground to flour.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. Rice(new).
2. Carom seeds.
3. Sesame seeds.
4. Salt.
5. Oil.

Item Quantity

1. 1 cup.
2. 1 tablespoon.
3. 1/4 cup.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. Soak new rice overnight and in the morning strain water and blend into fine powder.
2. Sew the rice flour with sewing mess and add carom seeds, sesame seeds, salt and mix it.
3. Now add water and mix some mixture with this to make a light dough.
4. Take a clean cotton fine cloth and take the dough in hand which should not stick to hand.
5. Take small portion of dough in hand and keep stretching and rotating it on cloth.
6. Rest this for 15 minutes so that it dries little bit.
7. In a hot oil deep fry these and take them out on a tissue paper when they turn golden.
