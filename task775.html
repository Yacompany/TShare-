Besan Chilla or Besan Cheela is a delicious and healthy savory Indian pancake recipe made with nutty gram flour (besan), sweet onions, tangy tomatoes, fragrant spices and herbs. Fill the batter with your favorite grated vegetables to make your meal more nutritious, and serve as a quick breakfast, brunch, or even as a late-night snack.-------------

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. rice.
2. urad dal.
3. Fenugreek seed.
4. Salt.

Item Quantity

1. 2 cup.
2. 1 cup.
3. 1 tbsp.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. Sock rice and urad dal overnight and fneegreek seed also
2. Next day grind it.
3. Make smooth paste.
4. Now take steamer add water and dein add idli mould in it.
5. Now steam for 15 mint and take out and serve with Chutney of different type and sambhar.
