MOONG DAAL KHICHDI

Moong dal khichdi is easy to digest and provides a good balance of protein, carbohydrates, and nutrients. Moong dal is a yellow-skinned lentil that's high in protein and fiber, and helps you feel full for longer.

Method Of Cocking

1. Wash and soak equal parts basmati rice and moong dal for 30 minutes.
2. In a pressure cooker or pot over medium heat, heat ghee and add cumin seeds and bay leaf.
3. When the seeds sputter, add ginger and sauté until fragrant.
4. Add onions and green chilies.
5. Add tomatoes, carrots, beans, salt, and turmeric.
6. Add the drained rice and dal.
7. Add 4 cups of water for a porridge-like consistency or 3 cups for a mushy consistency.
8. Cover and cook on medium flame for 2 whistles for a mushy khichdi.
9. Mash the khichdi slightly for a thicker consistency.
10. Serve with papad and pickle.
