BANANA DALIYA

This daliya recipe gives a unique twist to the usual recipe we eat. The dish is loved by everyone. It is healthy, nutritious, and a good option for weight loss. You can have it not only have it for breakfast but for snacks too.

Method Of Cocking

1. In a bowl, wash the broken wheat (daliya) thoroughly.
2. Add one glass of water and pressure cook it for 2 whistles.
3. Once done, let the daliya cool down at room temperature.
4. Cut the banana in roundels and apple into cubes.
5. Add the cooked daliya into a bowl.
6. Add the chopped banana and apple to the bowl.
7. Sprinkle sabja seeds, chia seeds, watermelon seeds and sunflower seeds as garnish. Enjoy the tasty yet crunchy super bowl.
