Method Of Cocking

1. Take out the gram flour in a bowl, add celery, turmeric and salt and mix. Chop onion, green chilli, curry leaves and coriander leaves. Mix everything together.
2. Now add water and prepare a thick batter. Add baking soda and mix. Now heat oil in a pan and add all the pakoras and fry them by turning them over and over until they turn golden brown.
3. Take it out in a bowl.
4. Serve with tomato chutney.
