SWEET POTATO GULAB JAMUN

Sweet potato gulab jamun is a North Indian dessert made from mashed sweet potatoes, flour, and a rose-flavored syrup. The dough balls are deep-fried and soaked in the syrup.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. 2 cups of mashed sweet potatoes.
2. 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour.
3. 2–3 tablespoons of sugar.
4. 300 grams of sweet potatoes.
5. 1 tablespoon of maida.
6. 1 cup of sugar.
7. 1 cup of boiled and mashed sweet potatoes.
8. 20 tablespoons of milk powder.
9. 2 cups of sugar.

Method Of Cocking

1. In a saucepan, combine sugar, water, cloves, and saffron thread and bring to a boil. Cook over high heat until the syrup thickens, about 5–7 minutes. Remove from heat and let it rest.
2. In a bowl, mash sweet potatoes until smooth and lump-free. Add baking soda, flour, milk, and knead until smooth. If the dough is too dry, add more milk, and if it's too sticky, add more flour.
3. Using a spoon, form small, equal-sized balls from the dough. Lightly grease your hands with ghee before shaping the balls.
4. Heat oil and add some ghee. Deep fry the balls until they are deep brown. Remove from heat and place on a paper towel.
5. While still warm, slide the balls into the warm sugar syrup.
