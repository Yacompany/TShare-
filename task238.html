CURD RICE

Curd rice, also known as dahi chawal or thayir sadam in Hindi and Tamil, is a dish that originated in South India and is eaten throughout the country. It's made with precooked rice, yogurt, herbs, and spices, and is often eaten with pickles.

Method Of Cocking

1. Wash and pressure cook rice with 1 ¾ cups of water for 5 whistles on medium heat.
2. Mash the rice well and mix in hot, boiled milk and butter.
3. Let the rice cool, then add yogurt and mix well.
4. Add other ingredients and mix well.
5. Temper with the remaining ingredients
