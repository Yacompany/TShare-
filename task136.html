IDLI WITH COCONET CHUTNEY

Idli is often served with coconut chutney, a condiment that originated in India and is commonly eaten with other South Indian breakfast dishes like dosa, vada, and upma.

Method Of Cocking

1. Add 1/2 cup of fresh grated or chopped coconut, 2 tablespoons of fried gram, 1/2 teaspoon of cumin, 1â€“2 green chilies, and salt to a blender or chutney jar.
2. Blend until well combined, scraping down the sides.
3. Add water as needed and blend until smooth and thick.
4. Taste and adjust the salt and chilies to taste.
5. Transfer to a serving bowl.
