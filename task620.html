KACHRI SABJI

The urad filled deep fried crispy kachoris is served along with flavour packed panchporan ( 5 spice mix from Bengal ) alu sabzi, makes this dish an absolute cracker! This recipe is very close to my heart and took me 1.5 years to master it.

Method Of Cocking

1. In a parat or a bowl bring all the contents of the dough together, maida, sooji, baking soda, dhaniya & saunf mix, hing and salt. Knead a soft dough using 1 cup water or a little more if required.
2. Flatten the dough and apply 2 tbspn of water by digging in pits with your finger. Cover the dough with a wet muslin cloth. (This process is done to avoid drying of the dough. Sooji swells up and hence requires a little extra water to keep the dough soft.) Let the dough rest for 1 hour.
3. Strain all the water from urad dal. And put it in a blender. Add salt and grind it into a coarse paste. (Without adding any water.) Empty its contents in a bowl and keep it aside.
4. In a deep kadai, heat 2 tbspn oil, put two bay leaves, Panchporan spice mix and chopped ginger. Saute this till ginger is slightly cooked.
5. Now add all the spices. Hing, pepper, kashmiri chilli powder, sabzi masala & dhaniya powder. Saute this for about 30 secs.
6. Add the roughly chopped potatoes and mix it well till the spices have coated the potatoes beautifully.
7. Now put 1Â½ cup water and let it simmer in low heat for about 3 to 4 minutes. Add kasuri methi and freshly chopped coriander leaves.
