DAL AND RICE

Dal chawal, or dal and rice, is a traditional North Indian dish that's a staple of the Indian diet. It's a complete meal that's high in fiber, low in calories, and fat free except for any added oil or ghee. Dal chawal is also a good source of protein, vitamins, calcium, and carbs.

Method Of Cocking

1. Heat oil in a tava. Add mustard seeds, urad dal,jeera and channa dal.
2. Let it splutter.
3. Add curry leaves, green chilles to it.
4. Chop the onions and tomatoes. Add them to tava.
5. Now add turmeric powder,salt to the mixture.
6. Saute for about minutes.Atlast add corrainder leaves to this 4 5.
7. Now take a thick bottomed vessel which is normally used for cooking rice. Add dal and rice. Use required amount of water.
8. To this add the sauteed mixture.Mix well.
9. Keep them in pressure cooker and wait for upto 4 whistles.
10. Dal and rice would have been cooked completely with other ingredients.
11. Dal rice is ready to served with Pappad or pickle.
