Method Of Cocking

1. In a bowl, combine 1 cup of chickpeas, 1 potato, 1 tsp green chutney, 1 tsp tamarind chutney, 2 tbsp curd, ¼ tsp salt, and ¼ tsp chili powder.
2. Transfer the mixture to a serving plate.
3. Crush 3 papdi over the mixture.
4. Top with 1 tbsp curd, 1 tsp green chutney, and 1 tsp tamarind chutney.
5. Add 1 tbsp onion and 1 tbsp tomato.
6. Sprinkle with a pinch of chili powder, cumin powder, aamchur, chaat masala, and salt.
7. Top with 3 tbsp sev, 1 tsp onion, and 1 tsp tomato
