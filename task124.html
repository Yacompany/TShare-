WONTON (CHINA)

Wontons are a type of Chinese dumpling that are a staple of Cantonese cooking and are popular in other East and Southeast Asian cuisines.Wontons are usually boiled and served in soup, but can also be deep-fried and enjoyed as an appetizer or side dish.

Method Of Cocking

1. Put a teaspoon of filling in the center of a square or round wonton wrapper.
2. Moisten the edges with water.
3. Fold the wonton in half to make a triangle.
4. Press the edges together to seal.
5. Bring the left and right corners together above the filling.
6. Overlap the tips of the corners, moisten with water, and press together to seal.
7. Add a little water along the edges to help the filling stick.
8. Place frozen wontons together so they're touching, but not too snug, so they don't stick together when frozen.
