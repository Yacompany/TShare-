OOTTITHOUGBA

Today our contributor Tushar from Manipur shares his favorite type of Uti Thongba called ‘Usoi Mangan uti’ (Whole grain with fresh bamboo shoot uti). Read on to find out how to make it.

Method Of Cocking

1. Heat up the vessel and put oil and wait for the timing
2. When it’s ready, add tejpata, zeera and methi.
3. After sometime add maroi nakuppi (chives) and stir it for a few good seconds.
4. Time to add manggan (yellow pea), water, sodium bicarbonate.
5. Make sure to add baking soda before it comes to a boil, most probably right after you add the water.
6. Now add usoi (fresh bamboo shoot) and green chillies.
7. Boil it until the peas get soft and the usoi to be cooked. Add salt as per taste.
