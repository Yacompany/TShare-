RHENGAN REVEYA

Ringan ravaiya is a popular Gujarati dish of stuffed eggplant made with a variety of spices and accompaniments. Here's a recipe for this healthy dish.Ringan ravaiya goes great with rice or nahatl tea.

Method Of Cocking

1. To prevent the eggplant from becoming mushy and greasy, you can salt sliced or diced eggplant and let it sit for an hour to draw out moisture. Then, rinse and drain any liquid, and pat dry before cooking.
2. The curry may seem watery at first, but it will thicken as it cooks.
3. To test if the eggplants are cooked, you can poke them with a knife or fork.
