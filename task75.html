UNDHIYA

Undhiyu is a flavorful Gujarati mixed vegetable dish that is a regional specialty of Surat, Gujarat, India. The name comes from the Gujarati word "undhu," which means "upside down," as the dish is traditionally cooked upside down underground in earthen pots called "matlu".
