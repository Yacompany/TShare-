CUCUMBER RAITA

Cucumber raita is a refreshing, savory condiment made with yogurt, cucumber, and other ingredients. It's often served with biryani or pulao to enhance the flavor of the dish.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

2. cucumbers, peeled, seeded, and thinly sliced
3. lemon juice.
4. chopped fresh mint.
5. white sugar.
6. kosher salt.

Method Of Cocking

1. Whisk chilled yogurt in a large bowl.
2. Add chopped cucumber, coriander leaves, mint leaves, and salt.
3. Mix well.
4. Taste and add more salt if needed.
5. Drizzle with cumin powder, ground black pepper, and chaat masala (optional).
