FULKA ROTLI

This Khaman Dhokla recipe makes a wonderfully soft and fluffy, lightly sweet, spiced and savory gram flour cake that’s perfect to enjoy anytime of the day. It’s easy to prepare, quick and instant Khaman recipe steamed on the stovetop or even in the Instant Pot with my step-by-step photos, video and instructions.

Method Of Cocking

1. Grease a steamer pan with 2 to 3 teaspoons oil.
2. Take 1.5 cups gram flour (120 grams besan) in a mixing bowl or pan. Do use gram flour which has a fine texture.
3. Add 1 cup water (or more or less as needed) and 1 tablespoon oil to make a thick yet smooth flowing batter.
4. Then add 1 tablespoon rava (semolina). This is entirely optional but adds a good texture to the khaman. Skip rava or semolina for a gluten-free option.
5. Stir with a whisk to create a smooth, thick batter without any lumps.
6. The batter should be thick yet flow easily off of the whisk. A quick tip is that if the batter becomes thin, then 1 to 2 tablespoons of gram flour.
7. Next add 2 teaspoons Eno or fruit salt. 2 teaspoons of Eno makes the khaman soft and fluffy. But there is a slightly alkaline taste to Eno.
8. Stir and mix the eno with the batter briskly and quickly.
9. The fruit salt should be mixed evenly with the batter. Or else you get uneven texture in the khaman.
10. The eno will make the batter become frothy, so you need to work quickly to whisk it in thoroughly.
