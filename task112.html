JHAL MUDI

Jhal muri is a popular, sweet, spicy, and tangy street food snack from Kolkata, India. The name comes from the Bengali words "jhal" which means "spicy" and "muri" which means "puffed rice".

Method Of Cocking

1. To a mixing bowl add tomatoes, cucumber, onions, potatoes, coriander leaves and green chilies.
2. Add salt,red chili powder, cumin powder and chaat masala.
3. Squeeze the lemon. Give them a good stir.
4. Add peanuts and puffed rice.
5. Toss them all.
6. Serve jhal muri immediately.
7. In a small bowl, mix roasted cumin powder, black salt, red chili powder, and chaat masala.
8. In a kadai over low or medium-low heat, add puffed rice and stir often until it's crunchy.
9. Boil potatoes, then peel and cut into 1 cm cubes. Finely chop onions, green chilies, and ginger. Dice the tomatoes, removing the pulpy interior.
