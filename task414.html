TOHAN MOSDENG

Mosdeng is a Tripuri food made of chilies. The chilies are usually burnt slightly over an open flame to give a smoky flavor. Mosdeng is a side dish prepared by grinding fresh roasted chilies and salt. Sometimes vegetables are added.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. Bone less Chicken.
2. Onion (medium size).
3. Ginger-Garlic Paste.
4. Green Chilies.
5. Dry Red Chilies.
6. Awa Fadigom (Wild Coriander).
7. Sichuan Pepper.
8. Salt.
9. Water.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. First wash the chicken and tear it into small pieces. Now crushed the Ginger and Garlic. Boil chicken in cooker with Sichuan Pepper for 20 minutes in medium flame.
2. While boiling, we now chopped Onion and Awa Fadigom in thin slices. Now roast green chillies and Dry Red Chillies and poundq it.
3. Mixing step. It's the most important part of Tripuri's Mosdeng too. Mix all paste of Green Chillies, Ginger and Garlic. Now add Onion and Dry Red Chillies. After that, we add our boil Chicken and mix it all very well and garnished it with Awa Fadigom and Roasted Sichuan Pepper.
4. Note : Generally, Sichuan Pepper are not used by Tripuri people in any Mosdeng, but we gave it a try and it turned out preety good.
