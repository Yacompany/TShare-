NGATOK

Ngatok is essentially a fish stew that also uses lemongrass and herbs. To make Ngatok, the fish is wrapped in leaves, cooked on hot stones and then minced. Local spices are added to the marinade used for the fish to give it flavour.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. Rice flour.
---------- 2. Bananas.
3. Jaggery , powdered.
4. Water.
5. Salt , a pinch.
6. Oil , for frying.

1. 1 cup.
2. 2 Ripe.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. To begin the preparation of Mizoram Style Koat Pitha, first dissolve the jaggery in water and pass it through a fine filter to remove the scum.
------ 2. Mash the bananas and keep them aside in a bowl.
3. To the mashed banana, now add the jaggery water and rice flour and with a pinch of salt.
4. Mix the Koat Pitha mixture until well combined and becomes a thick batter.
5. Heat a pan with oil on medium heat and spoon a small amount of batter into the oil.Fry the Koat Pitha till it becomes golden and drain it on a paper towel.
6. Serve the Mizoram style Koat Pitha as a delicious dessert after a light meal of Sana Thongpa and Tan Ngan, or as an evening tea time snack along with masala chai.
