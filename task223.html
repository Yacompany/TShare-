KAKDI KARONDA SALAD

Khamang kakdi- Koshimbir is Maharashtrian term for salad, many different combinations of vegetables are used to make different variety of koshimbirs .

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. Cucumber / Kheera / Kakdi.
2. Peanuts / Moongphali (Roasted).
3. Fresh Coconut, Grated.
4. Fresh Coriander / Cilantro / Hara Dhaniya chopped.
5. Chillies / Hari Mirch chopped.
6. Lemon Juice / Nimbu Ka Ras.
7. Sugar / Chini.

Item Quantity

1. 3 Medium size.
2. 4 tbsp.
3. 4 tbsp.
4. 4 tbsp.
5. 1 Green.
6. 1 tbsp.
7. 3/4 tsp.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. Rinse and peel the cucumbers and finely chop them.
2. Add little salt and keep aside for 10 minutes,so they will release the extra moisture.
3. Crush the peanuts coarsely in a mortar and pestle (don't grind in the mixer)
4. Now gently squeeze the cucumbers to remove the extra water.
5. In a bowl add the chopped cucumbers, coconut, peanuts,chopped green chili, fresh coriander and mix well
6. Heat oil or ghee in a small pan.
7. Add cumin ,mustard seeds ,curry leaves and asafoetida
8. When mustard seeds start to crackle then add this tempering in the salad and mix
9. Add lemon juice ,salt and sugar when you want to serve
