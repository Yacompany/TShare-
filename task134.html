VEGETABLE PULAO

Vegetable pulao is a popular rice dish from South Asia, Central Asia, and the Middle East that can be made with a variety of vegetables.You can also add garam masala powder, turmeric powder, and red chili powder.

Method Of Cocking

1. Slice onions long and shred vegetables.
2. Cook rice and set aside.
3. Heat oil or ghee in a wide pan and add onions.
4. When onions start to brown, add cinnamon, cloves, and cardamom.
5. Stir fry until onions are darker brown, then add minced garlic and fry a few seconds.
6. Add shredded vegetables and green chilies, mix with salt, and cook on low heat, covered, until vegetables are soft.
7. Mix in rice and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
8. Turn off heat and keep partially covered until ready to serve.
