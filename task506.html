CHAKULI PITHA

Come a special occasion and a typical Odia mind will go straight into the vast array of pithas that the rich and diverse Odia cuisine treasures. From weddings to pujas and festivals, pitha is a quintessential part of a typical Odia spread.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. Rice flour.
2. Black gram.
3. Fenugreek seeds.
4. Water.
5. Salt tsp.
6. Mustard oil.

Item Quantity

1. 2 cup.
2. 3/4 cup.
3. 1 pinch.
4. 2.50 cup.
5. 1.50 As.
6. 2-3 tablespoon.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. Soak the rice, black gram and fenugreek seeds overnight.
2. Once it's soaked, make a thick batter of rice, black gram, fenugreek seeds, salt to taste and water and leave it to ferment for 6-8 hours.
3. Heat the pan and grease with some oil.
4. Pour the batter into the pan and spread in a circular motion to make it round.
5. Spread some oil and cook it on both sides till perfectly crispy and cooked.
