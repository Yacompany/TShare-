MORINGA KI SABJI

Moringa or drumstick vegetable is a treasure trove of nutrients. Moringa vegetable, which is very beneficial for health, is beneficial in many diseases. It is especially used in bone pain. Regular consumption of Moringa also strengthens the liver and digestion.

Method Of Cocking

1. To make Moringa or Drumstick Sabzi, first peel the Moringa pods. After this, cut them into long pieces. After this, cut the potatoes into small pieces. Instead of cutting the tomatoes, make a big incision on them. Now put potatoes, tomatoes, Moringa pods, water and salt as per taste in a pressure cooker. Put the lid on the cooker and let it whistle 2-3 times and then turn off the gas.
2. Heat oil in a pan. When the oil is hot, add cumin seeds and crackle them for 8-10 seconds. After this, add finely chopped onion (if desired, onion paste) and let it cook on low flame. After some time, add coriander powder, turmeric, red chili powder and mix and let the masala cook. Meanwhile, open the cooker and strain the vegetables and use the water in the gravy.
3. After this, take tomatoes, peel them and fry them in onion masala. After cooking tomatoes for some time, add boiled drumstick pods and potatoes and mix. Then add the water of the pods as well. Add salt to the vegetable as per taste and let it cook. When the vegetable boils well, turn off the gas. The tasty and nutritious Moringa vegetable is ready. You can garnish it with green coriander leaves and eat it anytime for lunch or dinner.
