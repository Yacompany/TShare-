SATTU KI ROTI

Sattu paratha recipe is a healthy Indian flatbread with sattu flour along with other simple ingredients is stuffed inside wheat dough.

INGREDIENT'S

2. gram flour.
3. taste Salt.
4. chillies finely chopped.
5. onion finely chopped.
6. Ginger finely chopped.
7. Garlic cloves.
8. Ajwain - Black Cumin Mix.

4. 1 large.

6. 10-12.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. Knead the dough well and cover it and keep it aside for 5 minutes. Take all the ingredients in a bowl and mix well with the help of some water. Keep in mind that the mixture should be like a powder, not too wet or too dry.
2. Now make dough balls and fill the mixture in them little by little. Repeat this with all the balls. Now roll them on the rolling board with the help of a rolling pin in the shape of a roti.
3. Now bake it well from both the sides. Eat it hot with chutney, curd or vegetables.
