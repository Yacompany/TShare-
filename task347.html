Method Of Cocking

1. Take ¼ cup (60 g) of ghee in a bowl. Make sure the ghee is in a semi-solid state and not runny, melted or a cold solid block.
2. Beat the ghee with a spoon, spatula or a small wire whisk until it becomes smooth, light and fluffy. This will also lighten its colour.
3. Add ¼ cup (60 g) of cold or chilled fresh yogurt.
4. Then beat the curd with ghee with the help of a spoon, spatula or a small wire whisk.
5. Mix the cream until light and smooth.
6. Now take 2 cups (250 grams) of flour in a sieve. Keep it on the bowl itself. You can also sift the flour separately on a plate or tray.
7. Add 1 pinch of salt, 1 pinch of baking soda and ½ teaspoon baking powder.
8. Strain straight into the creamed mixture.
9. Using a spoon or spatula, lightly mix the flour with the ghee and curd.
10. Add cold water little by little and start mixing. In total, you can use 8 to 9 tablespoons of cold water.
11. Mix everything and make a soft dough. Do not knead. Just mix everything and make a dough. Cover the dough and keep it aside for 15 minutes.
12. Meanwhile, prepare the sugar syrup. Also, you can prepare the sugar syrup while frying.
13. Add half a cup of water.
14. Stir to mix.
