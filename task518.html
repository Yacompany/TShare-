AXONE

Axone (pronounced ‘Aakhuni’) is a fermented soybean paste with a very strong pungent smell, and it is one of the most popular spices from Nagaland, in the North East of India. It is indigenous to the ‘Sumi’ Naga tribe but is widely used across all Naga dishes.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. fermented soybean.
2. grated onion.
3. black sesame seed/perilla.
4. turmeric.
5. pork cut into small pieces.
6. sliced ginger.
7. Oil to fry.
8. Salt to taste.
9. Green/red chilies.

Item Quantity

1. 500 grams.
2. 1 cup.
3. 4 tablespoon.
4. teaspoon.
5. 2 pork.
6. 2 tablespoon.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. Mash the fermented soybean paste and keep aside.
2. Heat oil in a pan and add onion, and fry till translucent.
3. Add turmeric and salt
4. Add the soybean paste
5. Add salt to taste
6. Add the ginger slices just as you get the aroma of tungrymba
7. Add some water ( not too much, not too less )
8. Let it be cooked and boiled for 25-30 minutes.
9. Stir it every 2-3 minutes
10. When it becomes a thick paste, then you will know it is ready.
