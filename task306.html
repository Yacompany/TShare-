MALAIYO

Malaiyo is a sweet, frothy milk dessert that's a specialty of Varanasi, India. It's made with milk cream, sugar, and dry fruits, and is often flavored with saffron. The dessert is light and creamy, and has a yellow color that's similar to soap suds. Malaiyo is traditionally served in small earthen bowls and garnished with almonds and pistachios.

Method Of Cocking

1. Boil the milk and add in the saffron and simmer the milk till it reduces by almost half, stirring it occasionally...then add sugar, Cardamom and simmer for few more minutes and let it cool at room temperature. Cover and keep it in the fridge overnight.
2. To keep the chilled milk cool all time while we churn the MALAI out of it....put some chilled water or ice cubes in a bowl and keep the chilled milk pot in it, be careful to select a bowl in which the pot fits well. Now with the help of an electric stick hand blender churn the milk, the milk will start becoming frothy and the MALAI will start collecting on top of the milk. Churn it for about 5 -10 minutes till the Cloud of frothy MALAY becomes dense.
3. With the help of a spoon, put the Churned frothy MALAI in individual bowl, garnish it with edible silver leaf and pistachios and serve it immediately. If not consuming immediately keep it in the fridge otherwise it will start becoming runny...it is best when eaten fresh!!! Bon Appetite!!! Don't throw away the flavoured skimmed milk make different recipes with it.
