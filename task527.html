INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. pork innards (heart, liver, small intestine, spleen).
2. Few chunks of pork.
3. Local red chilly or King chilly.
4. cloves of garlic.
5. large ginger.
6. Szechuan peppercorns.
7. Chilly powder.
8. Guava leaves (this helps remove the intense smell of the innards).
9. Salt.

1. 1 kilo.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. The innards, especially the intestines have to be cleaned properly, so I recommend boiling them with water, salt, and guava leaves (or vinegar/lemongrass/lime leaves) for 5 minutes to remove the smell.
2. Take them out, place them on a strainer, and run cold water over it.
3. Cut them into bite-sized pieces
4. Tip: The secret to this amazing dish is throwing in some pork chunks to enhance the flavor.
5. Now put the chunks of innards and meat into a pot, add salt, and cook without water on high heat till it starts to produce its own lard.
6. After the innards are partially cooked, add mashed ginger, garlic, red chilly, king chilly (optional), chilly powder, and one cup of water.
7. Slow cook till the innards are well cooked, then add water little by little as required.
8. Once the meat is tender and fully cooked, increase the flame, and cook for a few more minutes until dry.
9. Finally, add more freshly sliced ginger, and roasted Szechuan peppercorns.
10. After the flavor has been incorporated, remove the pot from the flame and garnish with fresh green chilly.
