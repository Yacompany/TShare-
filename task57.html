TUDKIYA BHATH

Tudkiya Bhaat is a traditional rice dish of Himachal Pradesh. It is cooked on low flame. It is made from soaked rice, lentils, and aromatic spices. It is cooked with curd.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. Rice.
2. Potatoes.
3. Spices.
4. Other ingredients: Yogurt, masoor dal, onions, tomatoes, green chilies, poppy seeds, and mace.
5. You can also add: dagad (stone flower), anise with four stars, and ghee.
6. To prepare the potatoes, you can wash and slice them, then marinate them in a paste of ginger-garlic, coriander, cardamom, tomatoes, and onions.

Item Quantity

1. Two cups of basmati rice.
2. Two medium potatoes.
3. Spices: Freshly ground spice paste, including cardamom, cinnamon, bay leaf, coriander, ginger, garlic, and cloves.
4. Other ingredients: Yogurt, masoor dal, onions, tomatoes, green chilies, poppy seeds, and mace.
5. You can also add: dagad (stone flower), anise with four stars, and ghee.
6. To prepare the potatoes, onions.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. Grind ingredients: Grind the potatoes into a paste. You can also marinate the potatoes in a paste of ginger, garlic, coriander, cardamom, tomatoes, and onions.
2. Add paste to dish: Add the ground paste to the dish along with salt and other spices.
3. Sauté: Sauté the whole spices with onions.
4. Add other ingredients: Add the potatoes, masoor dal, and rice to the dish.
5. Stir fry: Stir fry the potatoes, masoor dal, and other ingredients until the oil separates.
6. Cook: Add 3 cups of warm water and salt to taste. Cover the cooker and cook on high for two whistles.
7. Let pressure drop: Let the pressure drop naturally before opening the cooker.
