MAKAHNA KHEER

To make Makhana Kheer, Makhana can also be roasted in ghee and ground coarsely. Makhana can also be crushed with a mortar and pestle.Makhana kheer is high in calcium and protein, and the fox nuts used to make it are a good source of soluble fiber.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. Phool makhana: 1–1.5 cups.
2. Milk: 2–2 cups.
3. Ghee: 2–4 teaspoons.
4. Sugar: 1/4–3 tablespoons.
5. Saffron: A few strands.
6. Cardamom: 1/4 teaspoon or 0.5 teaspoons.
7. Pistachios: 7 pieces.
8. Reserve 1/3 cup of makhana while heating the milk.
9. Heat 2 cups of milk in a saucepan or thick-bottomed pan and bring to a boil.

Method Of Cocking

1. Roast the makhana in ghee for a couple of minutes until it's crisp and breaks easily when crushed by hand.
2. You can also add cashews to the kheer and season with rock salt.
3. Soak saffron in warm milk for 10 minutes before adding it to the kheer.
4. Makhana kheer can be served hot or cold and garnished with chopped dry fruits.
5. You can also make makhana kheer in advance, let it cool, and refrigerate it for a couple of days.
6. Heat milk in a saucepan over medium-low to medium heat, stirring occasionally to prevent scorching.
7. Bring the milk to a boil, then add sugar and the ground makhana powder.
8. Scrape any evaporated milk solids from the sides of the pan and add them to the milk.
