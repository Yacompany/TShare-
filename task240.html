BESAN KA CHEELA

Besan Chilla or Besan Cheela is a delicious and healthy savory Indian pancake recipe made with nutty gram flour (besan), sweet onions, tangy tomatoes, fragrant spices and herbs. Fill the batter with your favorite grated vegetables to make your meal more nutritious.

INGREDIENT'S

Items No

1. besan (gram flour) – 100 grams.
2. finely chopped onions or 1 small onion.
3. chopped tomatoes or 1 small tomato.
4. coriander leaves (cilantro).
5. chopped green chilies or serrano pepper.
6. chopped ginger or ½ inch peeled ginger.
7. carom seeds (ajwain).
8. turmeric powder (ground turmeric).
9. red chili powder or cayenne pepper.
10. water or add as required.

Item Quantity

4. ¼ cup.

6. ½ teaspoon finely.
7. ½ teaspoon.
8. 2 to 3 pinches.
9. ¼ teaspoon.
10. ½ to ⅔ cup.

Equipment Name Used In Dish

Method Of Cocking

1. First, measure 1 cup of gram flour (besan) and pour it into a mixing bowl. Replace gram flour with chickpea flour if you do not have it.
2. Next, add in your chopped veggies and herbs. It’s important to make sure all of these ingredients are FINELY chopped, otherwise, it becomes difficult to spread the chilla in the pan.
3. Before you start to stir or combine your ingredients, first add half a cup of water to the besan cheela mixture.
4. Next use a wired whisk to combine all of the besan chilla ingredients. Then, add an additional 1 to 3 tablespoons of water, depending on the quality and texture of the besan.
5. Next continue to cook until the base gets light golden.
