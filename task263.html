Method Of Cocking

1. First of all fry the gum in ghee.
2. Now fry makhana in the same ghee.
3. Roast the poppy seeds in the remaining ghee on the lowest flame. Remember to take out the excess ghee if it is there. Keep stirring the poppy seeds until the colour changes slightly. For about 5-7 minutes.
4. Now add turmeric powder, almond pieces, chironji and grated dry coconut and fry for another 5 minutes.
5. Now your masala is ready, if you want you can also add cashew nuts, melon seeds in it.
6. Now in another pan, add 2 teaspoons of water along with jaggery and keep stirring continuously on low flame. When it melts completely, turn off the gas and add all the above spices and dry ginger powder to it. Mix it well and when it cools down a bit, make laddus from it.
