MISA MASS KORDOI TENGA

How to make Misa Maas Kordoi Tenga Recipe (Shrimp With Star Fruit Curry) To begin making Misa Maas Kordoi Tenga Recipe (Shrimp With Star Fruit Curry), rub salt and turmeric powder on shrimps. Heat oil in a wok/frying pan & add shrimps and just fry for 2 minutes. Take out and keep aside.

Method Of Cocking

1. Start by rubbing salt and turmeric powder on shrimps.
2. Heat oil in a frying pan fry shrimps for 2 minutes and keep aside.
3. Then take the frying pan and add dried red chili and mustard seeds, add garlic paste, fry for a bit.
4. Then add star fruit pieces and after stirring for some time add 1/2 a cup water, turmeric powder, sugar and salt.
5. Let it cook for about 5 minutes.
6. Then add fried shrimp and crushed tomato and cook for another 10 minutes.
7. Serve hot with rice.
