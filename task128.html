TOCACO (BRAZIL)

Taco soup is a hearty, flavorful soup that's made like a ground beef taco in broth.Taco soup can be made with beef, vegetable, or chicken broth. You can also top it with shredded cheese and sour cream.

Method Of Cocking

1. Taco soup can be made with ground beef, ground chicken, or ground turkey. If using ground beef, brown it in the skillet before adding it to a slow cooker to create a tender texture.
2. Taco seasoning is a key ingredient that gives the soup body and flavor, and most taco seasoning packets contain cornstarch as a thickening agent.
3. Canned diced tomatoes and Rotel diced tomatoes with green chilies can create a chili-like base with a bit of heat.
4. A few tablespoons of tomato paste can thicken the broth and add brightness to the soup.
5. Cilantro is a common herb in Mexican food and can be used to marinate meat or in soups.
6. Taco soup can be topped with your favorite taco toppings, or you can make it a spread so each person can build their own bowl.
