BABRU

Babru is a traditional, deep-fried flatbread from Himachal Pradesh, India that's stuffed with black gram or urad dal. It's often eaten for breakfast or as a snack at teatime.

INGREDIENT'S

3. 2 tablespoons ghee or clarified butter.
4. 75 grams brown sugar or jaggery powder.
5. 75 milliliters water.
6. 10 grams fennel seeds (optional).
7. Sunflower oil for deep frying.
