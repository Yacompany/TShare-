BAJRE KI LAPSI

In winters, jaggery, ghee, dry fruits and various new grains which were earlier included in our regular diet, are very beneficial for health. I have prepared this recipe in the way of my grandmother.

Method Of Cocking

1. Collect all the ingredients, put one spoon of ghee in the pan, fry all the dry fruits and take them out.
2. Now heat all the ghee in the same pan, add bay leaves and after a few seconds add millet flour and fry on low medium flame while stirring continuously.
3. On the other hand, put jaggery in a vessel with four times the amount of water and put it on the gas to melt; the jaggery just has to melt.
4. When the millet flour is roasted nicely, put a big sieve on the pan and pour the melted jaggery over it, this way if there is any impurity in the jaggery, it will get washed away.
5.Now mix the mixture on medium flame while continuously stirring it, when the mixture absorbs all the water, add salt, dry fruits, black pepper and cardamom powder, mix well and turn off the gas.
6. Bajra lapsi is ready, serve it hot.
