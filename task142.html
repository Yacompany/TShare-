CHEESE MASALA TOOST

Masala cheese toast is a flavorful snack made with toasted bread, cheese, and a variety of spices and other fillings.Masala cheese toast typically serves four people.

Method Of Cocking

1. Heat oil in a non-stick pan and add curry leaves and green chilies.
2. Add grated potatoes, turmeric powder, salt, and mix.
3. Cook for 1–2 minutes, then add coriander leaves and mix.
4. Transfer the mixture to a bowl.
5. Spread butter on one side of two bread slices.
6. Spread green chutney on one slice, and the potato mixture and cheese on the other.
7. Cover the chutney side with the other slice.
8. Heat a non-stick tawa.
