Method Of Cocking

1. Add salt to cut bush meat.
2. Pour hot Water on it to cover it.
3. Leave for 5-7mins.
4. Drain.
5. Cut out head and nails but you can peel out flesh from head,then add to meat.
6. Rinse well,season and cook with 3cups of water,cook for 7mins,set aside.
7. Heat up oil for stew,add tomatoes puree, stir in on med heat.
8. Now add all ingredients, including meat and stock, stir,well,bring to boil.
9. You may add water to your desired consistency,taste to adjust with salt and any spice of choice when it boils.
10. ook for maximum 8mins.
